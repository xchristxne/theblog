3. Redwood Place Cafe & Market

Nestled within the Redwood Place Apartment Homes in Sunnyvale, California, the Redwood Place Café & Market offers residents and visitors a unique blend of culinary delights and community ambiance. This establishment seamlessly integrates a café and market, providing a versatile space for dining, relaxation, and social gatherings.

Culinary Offerings

The café's menu caters to a wide array of tastes, serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Guests can indulge in acai bowls, paninis, gourmet salads, and other delectable dishes. Complementing the food, the café features six local beer and wine options on tap, pairing excellently with their charcuterie boards and fresh fare.

For those on the move, the on-site market offers grab-and-go sandwiches, snacks, and beverages. Coffee enthusiasts can enjoy freshly brewed Starbucks coffee, tea, and specialty drinks daily. The market's self-checkout system ensures quick and convenient service.

Ambiance and Design

The café boasts a cozy, rustic atmosphere with modern touches, making it an ideal spot for relaxation or work. Comfortable gathering areas, both indoors and outdoors, are designed with the community in mind. Patrons can watch games on large TVs or enjoy the heated outdoor patios, perfect for socializing, happy hours, or sharing a meal.

Location and Accessibility

Located at 1034 Indian Wells Avenue, Sunnyvale, CA 94085, the café provides convenient, dedicated parking within the apartment community, accommodating both dine-in and to-go orders.

Community Feedback

Patrons have praised the café's ambiance and offerings. One reviewer noted, "The café has such a cozy, rustic atmosphere with a modern touch that makes it the perfect spot to relax or get some work done."

Another guest highlighted the convenience and quality, stating, "Chill little marketplace with Starbucks coffee and tasty sandwiches and other light food."

Conclusion

Redwood Place Café & Market stands as a testament to thoughtful integration of dining and community space, offering a versatile environment for various occasions. Whether you're a resident seeking a local hangout or a visitor exploring Sunnyvale, this café and market provide a welcoming and enriching experience.
