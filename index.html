Caffé Bene
Best Overall Study Spot

If you're looking for a study-friendly café with a relaxed atmosphere, Caffé Bene in San Jose is a must-visit. Inspired by European café culture, this charming spot offers spacious seating, a warm ambiance, and a menu filled with delicious coffee, teas, and signature desserts like honey bread and waffles.

Menu: Offers breakfast, sandwhiches, signiture/classic coffees, smoothies, waffles & desserts.

Price range: $5-$15

Location: 181 E Tasman Dr. Ste 80, San Jose, CA. 95134

Living Room Coffee Craft
Best Spacious Study Spot

Nestled in the heart of Campbell, Living Room Coffee Craft is a welcoming café with a warm, homey ambiance—perfect for studying, working, or just unwinding with a great cup of coffee. With spacious seating, artisan coffee, and a peaceful atmosphere, this spot truly lives up to its name, making you feel right at home. Living Room Coffee Craft offers the perfect blend of comfort and productivity.

Menu: Offers signiture/classic coffees, herbal teas, paninis, avocado toasts, salads, & pastries.

Price range: $1-$10

Location: 1711 Winchester Blvd. Campbell, CA. 95008

Redwood Place Cafe
& Market
Best Upscale Study Spot

Redwood Place Café & Market offers a peaceful and inviting atmosphere perfect for studying or remote work. With a selection of fresh coffee, tea, and light bites, this cozy café provides a quiet retreat away from the usual hustle and bustle. Redwood Place Café delivers a comfortable space with friendly service and a laid-back vibe.

Menu: Offers Breakfast, deli, flatbread, small plates, & signiture/classic coffee.

Price range: $2-$16

Location: 1034 Indian Wells, Sunnyvale, CA. 94085
